🔴 Forbidden
1. Violation of license terms (see /about for details);

⚖️ Sanctions

The severity of punishment depends on the seriousness of the violation and the damage caused — from temporary restrictions to permanent account bans.

The administration reserves the right to restrict access to the project for any player who violates the rules or acts against fair play principles, even if such actions are not explicitly listed above.

In case of significant harm to the project, the administration reserves the right to protect its interests through legal channels.
